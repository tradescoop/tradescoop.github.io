# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 21, 2025

Tradescoop (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal data.  
This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how we collect, use, and disclose information through our websites, mobile applications, and other online services (collectively, the “Services”).  
If you have questions, please contact us.

---

# 1. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways:

---

## 1.1 Information You Provide
When you sign up for or use Tradescoop, you voluntarily provide information, such as:
- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Photos  
- Trade ideas  
- Comments  
- Other user-submitted content  

You may also share your location via device settings. If not shared, we may still infer approximate location through your IP address.

---

## 1.2 Log Data
When using Tradescoop, our servers automatically record information including:
- IP address (used to infer approximate location)  
- Browser type and version  
- Search activity  
- Pages visited  
- Date and time of requests  
- Time spent on pages  
- Other browsing statistics  

This information is used to:
- Understand user interaction and improve Services  
- Protect systems from abuse  
- Monitor and resolve potential security issues  

---

## 1.3 Device Information
We collect data about your device, including:
- Device type  
- Operating system  
- Settings  
- Unique device identifiers  

This helps diagnose issues and improve functionality.

---

## 1.4 Cookie Data
We use cookies and similar technologies to collect log data and enhance your experience.

Types of cookies:
- **Session cookies** (deleted when you close your browser)  
- **Persistent cookies** (remain until deleted)

Cookies are used to:
- Store preferences  
- Maintain settings  
- Improve performance  

You may refuse cookies, but some features may not function properly.

---

## 1.5 Clickstream Data & Inferences
We analyze your activity on Tradescoop, such as:
- Trade ideas you view  
- Trade ideas you create  
- On-platform interactions  

Combined with signup information and partner data, we generate inferences about interests and preferences.

---

## 1.6 Information from Partners & Advertisers
We receive additional data from:
- Affiliates  
- Advertisers  
- Partners  
- Third-party services  

This may include:
- Ad performance metrics  
- Visits to advertisers' sites  
- Purchases made on partner sites  
- Interest-based data used to personalize ad experiences  

---

# 2. How We Use Information

We retain information only as long as necessary to:
- Provide the Services  
- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy  
- Comply with legal or regulatory obligations  

When no longer needed, information is:
- Deleted from our systems, or  
- Depersonalized so it cannot identify you  

---

# 3. Children’s Information

- **Children under 13** are not permitted to use Tradescoop.  
- If you are in the **EEA**, you may only use Tradescoop if you are above the age required to provide lawful consent under your country’s data-processing laws.

---

# 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy occasionally.  
- Changes will be posted on this page.  
- Continued use of Tradescoop indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.  
- Significant changes may include prominent notice or require consent where legally required.
