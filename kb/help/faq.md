# Tradescoop FAQ

## Social Trading

### How does social trading work?
- Social trading enables users to **follow and learn from successful traders**.  
- You can view:
  - **Verified performance data**
  - **Trading strategies**
  - **Historical results**
- Users may also copy or model their trades.
- Functions like having a transparent mentor who shares both insights and track record.

## Safety & Security

### Is Tradescoop safe to use?
- Hosted on **Google Cloud Platform** with enterprise-grade security.  
- Tradescoop:
  - Does **not** handle user funds  
  - Does **not** access trading accounts  
  - Secures personal data using **bank-level encryption**  
- Shared content and user information remain protected at all times.

### Is my trading data secure and private?
- Tradescoop uses **end-to-end encryption** and rigorous data protection.  
- Only performance metrics or trade ideas you intentionally share become visible.  
- Personal trading details are **never sold or disclosed** to third parties.

## Experience Levels

### Do I need trading experience to use Tradescoop?
- Suitable for **all experience levels**, including beginners.  
- New users can:
  - Follow experienced traders  
  - Learn proven strategies  
  - Use the AI assistant for market analysis  
  - Explore in **demo mode** without risk  

### What if I’m new to trading?
- Beginners can study the feed, follow top performers, and ask the AI assistant for explanations.  
- The environment emphasizes **education** and **supportive learning**.

### How do I get started as a beginner?
1. Browse ideas in demo mode.  
2. Follow a few successful traders.  
3. Use the AI assistant for questions, summaries, and explanations.  
4. Create a free account when ready.  
5. Begin with **small positions** while learning the platform.
