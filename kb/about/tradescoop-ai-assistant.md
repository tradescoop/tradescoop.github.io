# Tradescoop Assistant Overview

## Summary
The **Tradescoop Assistant** is a conversational AI agent that combines **technical analysis**, **natural language understanding**, and **trade management capabilities** to deliver real-time, intelligent trading insights. The assistant analyzes markets, generates trade ideas, manages positions, and communicates through natural, context-aware conversation.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Market Analysis
- Analyzes **stocks** and **options** using technical indicators  
- Provides **real-time market insights** and trading signals  
- Integrates **news sentiment analysis** with technical data  
- Generates **comprehensive market reports**

---

### Trade Management
- Creates trading ideas from **natural language descriptions**  
- Updates and manages **active positions**  
- Closes trades and tracks **performance over time**  
- Publishes verified trade ideas directly to the **Tradescoop platform**

---

### Conversational Interface
- Understands and processes **natural language trading requests**  
- Maintains conversation **context and history**  
- Responds intelligently to market-related questions  
- Adapts to user **preferences and interaction patterns**

---

## Advanced Features

### Smart Summarization
- Summarizes long conversations and relevant insights  
- Extracts actionable information for rapid review  

### Security & Controls
- Implements **rate limiting** and platform protection features  
- Includes **health monitoring** and automated **error recovery**  
- Uses **JWT-based authentication** for secure communication  

---

## Important Relationships
- **Natural language → Trade idea generation → Automated publishing**  
- **Technical + Sentiment analysis → Real-time insights → Better decisions**  
- **Conversation history → Personalization → Improved user experience**
