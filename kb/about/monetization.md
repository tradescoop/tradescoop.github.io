# Monetization

## Summary
Tradescoop enables creators to earn income directly from the value they deliver to the trading community. Earnings are driven by engagement, subscriptions, and high-quality insights.

---

# 1. Creator Rewards

## 1.1 Engagement-Based Earnings
Creators earn rewards when their posts generate meaningful engagement, including:
- Reactions  
- Comments  
- Follows  
- Idea performance-driven visibility  

High-quality insights receive greater exposure and higher reward potential.

---

# 2. Creator Revenue Share

## 2.1 Subscription-Based Monetization
Creators can offer **monthly subscription access** to their content.  
Users may subscribe to:
- Premium trade ideas  
- Exclusive insights  
- Advanced strategies  
- Educational breakdowns  

Revenue is shared directly with creators, enabling them to sustainably earn from their expertise.

---

# Important Relationships
- **High-quality insights → More engagement → Greater rewards**  
- **Strong community reputation → More subscribers → Higher revenue**
