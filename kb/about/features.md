# Tradescoop Features

## Summary
Tradescoop is a social trading platform built around verified performance, transparency, and community-driven insights. The platform offers tools for discovering trade ideas, analyzing performance, engaging with traders, and earning rewards.

---

# 1. Trading Ideas & Discovery

## 1.1 Explore Verified Trade Ideas
- Browse real trades across **stocks, options, and crypto**  
- View **entry, exit, ROI, win rate, and risk metrics**  
- Filter by trader, asset class, time period, or strategy type  

## 1.2 Trade Idea Details
Each idea includes:
- Market context  
- Strategy explanation  
- Real-time performance updates  
- Historical accuracy of the trader  

---

# 2. Social Trading & Community

## 2.1 Follow Top Traders
- Follow users with proven performance  
- Receive real-time notifications when they post ideas  
- Analyze their track record transparently  

## 2.2 Community Engagement
- Comment on ideas  
- React and share feedback  
- Learn from diverse trading styles and perspectives

# 3. Proof-of-Profit System

## 3.1 Verified Performance Tracking
- Automatically tracks every trade from open to close  
- Displays:
  - ROI  
  - Drawdown  
  - Win rate  
  - Sharpe ratio  
  - Risk-adjusted performance  

## 3.2 Leaderboards
- Rank traders by verified performance  
- Filter by:
  - Asset class  
  - Strategy  
  - Risk level  
  - Timeframe
