# What Is Tradescoop

## Summary  
**Tradescoop** is a social trading platform where traders share **verified trade ideas**, track **real performance**, and **monetize insights**. It combines transparency, performance data, AI-powered insights, and token-based rewards into one accountability-driven trading ecosystem.

---

## Core Functions

### Explore Verified Ideas
- Browse **real trading ideas** from other investors across **stocks, options, and crypto**.  
- Each idea includes:
  - **Entry and exit data**  
  - **Performance metrics**  
  - **Verification** (on-chain or platform-tracked)  
- All results displayed in a single, transparent interface.

### Share Your Own Trades
- Post trading ideas with details:
  - **Symbol**
  - **Direction**
  - **Strategy**
- Tradescoop automatically tracks outcomes once closed.
- Performance is proven through transparent result tracking.

### Build and Showcase Reputation
- Profiles grow as trades perform.  
- Others can:
  - Follow you  
  - React to your ideas  
  - Subscribe to be notified when you post  
- Reputation is tied to **verified performance**, not noise.

### Monetize Insights
- Top traders can earn through:
  - **Paid followers**
  - **Token rewards**
  - **Leaderboard visibility**, highlighting verified performance

### Learn From Proven Traders
- Every idea includes **context and reasoning**.  
- Users see *how* top traders think, enabling strategy development and skill improvement.

---

## Why Tradescoop Is Different

### Accountability First
- Unlike traditional social platforms, Tradescoop enforces **transparency**.  
- Every idea is tied to **real, verifiable results**, reducing noise and increasing trust.

### A New Social Trading Ecosystem
- Combines:
  - **Real-time performance tracking**
  - **AI-powered insights**
  - **Token-based reward system**
- Focuses on **proven, high-quality ideas**, not speculation or unverified claims.

---

## Key Concepts

### Verified Trades
Trades are validated either **on-chain** or through **platform tracking**, ensuring authenticity and accuracy.

### Proof-Driven Reputation
User credibility is earned solely through **documented performance**.

### Incentivized Expertise
Reward mechanisms encourage sharing **high-quality insights**, benefiting both traders and followers.

- **Analysts** showcasing performance with proof.
