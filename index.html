About Tradescoop

Tradescoop is a cutting-edge social trading platform that leverages Web3, AI-powered trading agents, and blockchain transparency to enhance user collaboration and provide real-time insights. With an innovative ecosystem, Tradescoop empowers traders to maximize profitability, reduce risk, and tap into the collective expertise of the community.
